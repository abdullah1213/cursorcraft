Sharing work made easy

SparkleShare shines by its absence. Unlike other syncing tools it's designed to get out of your way, to make sharing documents and collaboration easier, and to make peers aware of what you are doing.

Built-in version control

SparkleShare keeps a record of all the changes in your files. Did you or someone else make a mistake? No problem, you can easily go back to an earlier version of that file.

Fast and secure

SparkleShare uses proven security technologies to keep your data safe whilst being transported, and because you can host your own service you will always be in control of the software and your data

Free as in Freedom

Not only doesn't SparkleShare cost you anything, you also have access to the full source code of it. You are free to use it for any purpose and modify it to fit your needs. These are only two examples of the freedoms you get when using Free Software.
